🛡️ Safety Notice

Results are cumulative dose indices and estimated effective dose for decision support — not the patient's exact radiation or organ dose.

True patient dose depends on size, anatomy, protocol, geometry and reconstruction. Patient dose estimation is complex and should involve a medical physicist.
